Elithair FAQ

1. Why do patients worldwide trust Elithair for hair restoration?

Elithair has restored the hair of over 150,000 patients from more than 70 countries. With internationally certified clinics, innovative techniques, and a 98–99% patient recommendation rate, we are trusted globally for safety, natural results, and compassionate care.

2. How many patients has Elithair treated successfully?

Elithair has successfully performed over 150,000 hair transplants. Our extensive experience ensures that every patient receives the highest standard of care and the best possible results.

3. Is the hair transplant procedure painful?

Thanks to our exclusive Sleep Deep Method (SDM), the procedure is virtually pain-free. Patients are placed in a twilight sleep for the local anesthesia, ensuring maximum comfort throughout the process.

4. What is the success rate of Elithair transplants?

We pride ourselves on a high success rate, with a 98-99% patient recommendation rate. Our use of advanced techniques like FUE and DHI ensures optimal graft survival and natural-looking density.
